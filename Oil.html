Title: Oil

Author: Butterfly Books / Lebanon Library

Summary of the Book:

Oil is essential in our lives, and we use it in many ways, including generating electricity to light homes and cities, powering factory machines, and heating schools and offices. Trucks, ships, and some types of locomotives use oil as fuel. Oil has many derivatives, including gasoline, which contributes to the movement of cars, and various oils that facilitate the operation of certain machine parts.
